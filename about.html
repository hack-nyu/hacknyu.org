About

HackNYU is a 48-hour hackathon taking place this March 23–25th, hosted at NYU locations around the world. Come to code, learn, and win prizes. This year, we have four hack categories for you to build for: Accessibility & Assistive Technology, Education Technology, Healthcare, and Sustainability & Social Impact. HackNYU is free, and made possible thanks to our wonderful sponsors and volunteers.

FAQ

Who can participate?

Anyone in high school, or college — even graduate students can attend our Brooklyn event! If you're under 18, just make sure you bring your liability waiver. However, attendees in Shanghai and Abu Dabi must be enrolled at NYU.

Where is HackNYU?

HackNYU is primarily taking place at NYU Tandon's __ Metrotech Center, which is easily accessible via the subway (Metrotech). Students enrolled in NYU Shanghai and Abu Dabi can attend their local events.

Will there be travel reimbursements?

Unfortunately, we won't be able to provide travel reimbursements this year.

How cool is HackNYU?
